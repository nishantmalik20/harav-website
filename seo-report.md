## ⚠️ Action before going live

1. **Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL=https://haravsalonspa.ca` in Vercel.** Right now
   (local dev) the sitemap, robots, canonicals and schema use `localhost:3000`.
   They switch to the real domain automatically once that env var is set in production.
2. **Fake ratings in Product schema.** Product pages now emit an
   `AggregateRating` built from the **placeholder** ratings (`lib/ratings.ts`).
   This feeds invented review counts to Google — a sharper version of the
   Competition Act / FTC concern than the visual stars alone. **Before launch:**
   wire real reviews, or remove `aggregateRating` from `productSchema()` (and the
   visual stars). One-line change.
3. **Real OG image (optional).** The generated card is clean; a photographed
   1200×630 card would be a nice upgrade later.
